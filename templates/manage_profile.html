Donor Ranking System

Our donor ranking system recognizes and celebrates your generosity. As you contribute more, you'll advance through different tiers, each with its own unique badge and recognition.

Bronze Supporter

₱50 - ₱999

Every journey begins with a single step. Thank you for starting yours with us!

Silver Supporter

₱1,000 - ₱4,999

Your continued support makes a meaningful difference in our community.

Gold Supporter

₱5,000 - ₱9,999

Your generosity shines bright and helps us reach more people in need.

Platinum Supporter

₱10,000 - ₱24,999

An exceptional supporter whose impact creates lasting change.

Diamond Supporter

₱25,000 - ₱49,999

A rare gem whose extraordinary generosity transforms lives.

Champion of Faith

₱50,000+

The highest honor - a true champion whose boundless generosity inspires us all.

Note: Rankings are based on your total completed donations across all parishes. Your rank advances automatically as you contribute more.
