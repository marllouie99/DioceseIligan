Help Center

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" tab on the landing page, fill in your details including your full name, email, and password. You'll receive a verification code via email to complete your registration.

How do I sign in?

Use the "Sign In" tab on the landing page and enter your email and password. You can also use the "Login with Code" option for passwordless authentication.

For Parishioners

How do I discover parishes?

Navigate to the "Discover Parishes" section from the landing page or dashboard. You can browse parishes in Iligan City and view their details, location, and activities.

How do I book a service?

Visit a parish page, navigate to the "Services" section, select the service you need (baptism, wedding, confession, etc.), choose an available time slot, and complete the booking form.

How do I make a donation?

You can donate goods or money to parishes through posts marked as "Fundraiser" or directly on a parish's page. Money donations are processed securely through PayPal or Stripe. The platform helps coordinate, track, and distribute donations for parish-led outreach programs.

How do I follow a parish?

Visit the parish page and click the "Follow" button. You'll receive updates about their posts, events, and activities in your feed.

For Parish Administrators

How do I create a parish?

Click on "Create Church" from the landing page or dashboard. Fill in the required information including parish name, description, location, and contact details.

How do I manage bookings?

Access the "Bookings" section from your parish management dashboard. You can view, approve, reschedule, or cancel bookings as needed.

How do I create posts and bulletins?

From your parish dashboard, navigate to "Posts" and click "Create Post". You can create general posts, digital bulletins, announcements, sermon summaries, events, or fundraiser posts with donation goals. This built-in feature provides a centralized alternative to third-party social media platforms.

How do I add staff members?

Go to your parish settings, navigate to "Team Management", and add staff members by their email. You can assign roles such as Manager or Staff.

How do I set up payment for donations?

In your parish settings, navigate to "Payment Information" and add your PayPal email address. This enables donation features on your posts.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I update my profile?

Navigate to your profile page from the dashboard and click "Edit Profile". You can update your personal information, profile picture, and bio.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we implement robust security measures including email verification, IP tracking, rate limiting, and secure payment processing to protect your data.
