2. Description of Service

Diocese of Iligan is a web-based platform designed to streamline parish administration and enhance community engagement for Roman Catholic parishes in Iligan City, Philippines. Our services include:

  • Parish profile management and discovery
  • Service booking system (baptism, weddings, confessions, etc.) with intelligent scheduling and conflict detection
  • Donation management for goods and money with secure payment processing
  • Coordination, tracking, and distribution of donations for parish-led outreach programs
  • Event reservation and coordination tools
  • Digital bulletins, announcements, and sermon summaries
  • Community engagement features (posts, comments, notifications)
  • Analytics dashboards and reporting tools for parish operations
